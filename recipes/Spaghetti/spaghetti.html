Spaghetti

Home

A classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti, crispy pancetta, eggs, and Parmesan cheese. It's rich, creamy, and ready in about 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 220 C
  2. Roll out the pizza dough
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough
  4. Add slices of mozzarella cheese
  5. Drizzle with olive oil
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes
  7. Top with fresh basil leaves before serving
